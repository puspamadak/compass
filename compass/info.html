Note: The accuracy of this compass depends on your device's sensors. Here are some tips to improve accuracy:
  • Rotate your device in figure 8 shape
  • Keep your device in horizontal position with the screen facing up (facing down won't work)
  • Keep your device away from metallic objects and other electronic gadgets

Interface help:
  • The compass will be shown only if your device have a magnetometer sensor
  • Click on the button to show info about this app
  • Click on the or icon to change the theme
